Introduction to Space

Welcome to the fascinating world of space exploration. This section provides a brief overview of the wonders beyond our planet.

Planets in Our Solar System

  1. Mercury
    • The closest planet to the Sun
    • Surface temperature ranges from extremely hot to extremely cold
  2. Venus
    • Known for its thick and toxic atmosphere
    • Similar in size and mass to Earth
  3. Earth
    • Our home planet, the only known celestial body with life
    • Abundant in diverse ecosystems

Exploring Galaxies

Galaxies are vast systems containing stars, planets, and various celestial objects. The study of galaxies unveils the mysteries of our expansive universe.
